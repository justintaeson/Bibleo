About Bibleo:

Bibleo is a place where the Bible and your personal story comes together. Bilbeo is an application that gives you the verse of the day and allows you to journal on the verse that has been given.

Here at Bibleo, we believe in the power of the Bible and the power of journaling.

To get started, you can either click on the button above or on the generate button in the header to generate your verse of the day.
